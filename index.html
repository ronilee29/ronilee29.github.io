Can i give you a call baby?

By: Roni Lee

two weeks ago that was you and me baby is someone else calling you baby goodbye's never easy

don't try to save me can i call you baby? can you be my friend?

let me show you love i’ll be there, whenever you want me i need somebody who can love me at my worst I know i’m not perfect

an actual baby or in a relationship? i never call humans that, just my cat.
